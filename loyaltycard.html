Get Points by signing up for our Local Garden Loyalty Program

Local Garden Loyalty Program Members reunite!

Shopping with us always has to be worthwhile, and that is why we give to you our Local Garden Loyalty Program where you earn points as you shop. This program is a point based system where you, as our customers, can enroll to collect points as you shop and eventually get lots of goodies with these points! The Local Garden Loyalty Program has approximately 17,000 people enrolled - each of them earning points as they shop.

How does the Local Garden Loyalty Program work?

It's simple - Kshs 100 = 1 point. So the more you shop, the more you can earn!
You can keep tabs on your points by checking on your receipts, as points get added everytime you shop with us.
As you continue to shop with us and collect your points, your points may be redeemable, too.

How can I sign up for the Local Garden Loyalty Program?

To sign up online, simply visit www.localgarden.co.ke and click on our Loyalty Card Registration section, fill in a few basic details and you're good to go. Once you've signed up, collecting points couldn't be easier and does not require a card or app.

All you have to do is give us your phone number - the one that you signed up with - at the till and your points will be added to your account within seconds. This helps to avoid the use of unnecessary plastic, which help us inch closer to our goal to go-green and make the world a better place one step at a time.

As a little thank you to our Loyalty Program members, we also have double points and double redemption every Monday and Wednesday. So, come over to any of our stores or shop online at www.localgarden.co.ke and shop until your trolley is full.

We appreciate our customers and continue to work on our loyalty program, so keep an eye out for our regular points giveaways and redemption offers!
